## Background

I am a postdoctoral fellow with the CNS program, and my background is in cognitive and computational neuroscience. 

## Project

My project is titled "Predicting treatment response and relapse in major depression using EEG signatures of neural attractor states". I'll be working with two CAN-BIND datasets to evaluate whether treatment resistance and relapse can be attributable to neural attractor states in depression.
